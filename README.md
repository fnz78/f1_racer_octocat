## 💡 Inspiration & Description

The inspiration behind **Racertocat** comes from combining the fast-paced, high-performance world of Formula 1 racing with the agile, collaborative nature of software development. Just like an F1 pit crew executing a lightning-fast tire change, developers rely on seamless collaboration, automation, and speed to deploy code. 

Mona is depicted wearing a specialized racing suit featuring GitHub-inspired sponsor badges, complete with her helmet, showcasing her ready to accelerate past merge conflicts and drive straight toward the checkered flag.
