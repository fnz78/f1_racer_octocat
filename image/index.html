🏎️ Racertocat

The F1 GitHub Mascot

💡 Inspiration

The inspiration behind Racertocat comes from combining the fast-paced, high-performance world of Formula 1 racing with the agile, collaborative nature of software development. Mona is depicted wearing a specialized racing suit featuring GitHub-inspired sponsor badges, complete with her helmet, showcasing her ready to accelerate past merge conflicts and drive straight toward the checkered flag.
